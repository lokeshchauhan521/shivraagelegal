Prevention of Sexual Harassment - Comprehensive Implementation & Support

What is POSH Compliance?

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) of Women at Workplace Act, 2013 is critical legislation that mandates organizations to create and maintain safe, harassment-free workplaces.

Policy Development

We create comprehensive POSH policies tailored to your organization's unique needs and requirements, ensuring full compliance with legal standards.

Implementation Support

From Internal Committee formation to documentation systems, we guide your organization through every step of POSH implementation.

Ongoing Compliance

Regular audits, training updates, and compliance reviews ensure your organization maintains adherence to all POSH requirements.

Training & Awareness Programs

Effective POSH compliance requires organizational awareness and understanding

Employee Training

Help all employees understand harassment, prevention strategies, and reporting procedures to create a safer workplace culture.

Management Programs

Equip supervisors and leaders with knowledge about their responsibilities and documentation requirements under POSH.

Committee Training

Prepare Internal Committee members with investigation procedures, documentation standards, and confidentiality protocols.

HR Team Support

The POSH Compliance Process

A systematic approach to ensure your organization is fully compliant

  1. Assessment & Diagnosis

    We evaluate your current policies, procedures, and organizational readiness for POSH compliance.

  2. Policy Development

    Comprehensive POSH policies are drafted, customized to your industry and organization size.

  3. Committee Formation

    We assist in identifying, selecting, and formally constituting the Internal Committee with proper representation.

  4. Training Implementation

    Organization-wide training sessions ensure all employees understand their rights and responsibilities.

  5. Documentation Systems

    Robust systems are established for complaint handling, investigation records, and compliance documentation.

  6. Ongoing Support

    Regular audits, policy updates based on regulatory changes, and continuous compliance monitoring.
